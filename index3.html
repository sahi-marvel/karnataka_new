Famous Foods of Karnataka

Masala Dosa

A crispy rice pancake filled with spiced potato, served with chutney and sambhar.

  • Masala Dosa
  • Ragi Mudde
  • Mysore Pak
  • Coorg Pork Curry

Ragi Mudde

Finger millet balls served with spicy curry, a nutritious Karnataka staple.

Mysore Pak

A sweet treat made of ghee, sugar, and gram flour, originating from Mysore.

Traditional Dances of Karnataka

Yakshagana

A vibrant folk theatre combining dance, music, and dialogue.

Dollu Kunitha

A drum-based dance performed by men in a group.
